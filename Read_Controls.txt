Compile/Run:
javac *.java
java Main

Controls:

R: right
L: left
D: Down
U: Up

Q : Backwards
W: Forwards
Up arrow: rotate along horizontal
Down arrow: rotate along horizontal
Left arrow: rotate along vertical
Right arrow: rotate along vertical

F: Controls shading/line display
Z: Z buffer

1: Selects triangle
2: Selects second triangle
3: Selects third triangle
K: Resets triangles
